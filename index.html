McNulty was raised in the Lauraville neighborhood of Baltimore, Maryland. McNulty's father worked as a steelworker at Bethlehem Steel until deindustrialization led to his firing in 1973. McNulty attended Loyola University Maryland for a year. McNulty's girlfriend, Elena, became pregnant, and he decided to join the Baltimore Police Department on April 5, 1994. He was an exemplary patrolman Western District under the command of Major Howard "Bunny" Colvin. In 2000, McNulty helped Detective Ray Cole solve a homicide case. He was reassigned to the Homicide Unit and was partnered with William "Bunk" Moreland. Elena divorced McNulty when Elena discovered McNulty's affair with Assistant State's Attorney Rhonda Pearlman. McNulty investigated "Little Kevin's" murder of Gerard Bogue. The State's Attorney's Office indicted Kevin, but he was acquitted after Barksdale Organization members used witness tampering. Kevin's acquittal impels McNulty to investigate their outfit further. He discovers their organization committed ten murders and secured three acquittals
